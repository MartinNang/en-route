Cookie Policy

Welcome to enRoute! We use cookies to enhance your experience, personalize content, and analyze site traffic. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your browsing experience. They enable essential functions, remember your preferences, and help us understand how users interact with our site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly, such as keeping you logged in and saving your itinerary preferences.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us analyze how users interact with our platform, allowing us to improve features and optimize your travel planning experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as preferred destinations or recently viewed itineraries, to provide a personalized experience.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser
